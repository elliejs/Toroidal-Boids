Torroidal Boids (Borgs)

        Camera Control:
        Right/Left arrow:         +/- x position
        Up/Down arrow:            +/- y position
        W/S:                      +/- z position

        Separation, Alignment, Cohesion Control:
        Q/A:     +/-      total importance: 0.01
        E/D:     +/- separation importance: 1
        R/F:     +/-  alignment importance: 1
        T/G:     +/-   cohesion importance: 1

        Neighborhood Control:
        Y/H:     +/-     inner ring radius: 0.1
        U/J:     +/-     outer ring radius: 0.2

        Other Control:
        Z:                      reset simulation
